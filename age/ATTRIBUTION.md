# Asset Attribution

All art in this project comes from **Kenney** (https://kenney.nl) and is released
under the **Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0)** public-domain dedication
(https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

CC0 does not *require* attribution, but Kenney asks to be credited, and we are
happy to:

> Art by **Kenney** — https://kenney.nl — used under CC0 1.0.

## Packs used

| Pack | Used for | Source | License |
|------|----------|--------|---------|
| Tiny Town (1.1) | terrain, houses, towers, trees, props (the "cities") | https://kenney.nl/assets/tiny-town | CC0 1.0 |
| Tiny Dungeon (1.0) | villager / unit character sprites | https://kenney.nl/assets/tiny-dungeon | CC0 1.0 |

Each pack's original `License.txt` is kept alongside its art under
`assets/kenney_tiny_town/` and `assets/kenney_tiny_dungeon/`.

The artwork has not been modified; we sample 16×16 tiles directly from each pack's
`tilemap_packed.png` atlas at runtime.
