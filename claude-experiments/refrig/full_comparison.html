Key Decision Factors

üìè Standard Depth vs Counter Depth

Standard Depth (36"+ deep): More storage capacity but protrudes beyond counters. Best if you prioritize maximum space.

Counter Depth (30-32" deep): Sits flush with cabinets for a built-in look but 10-20% less capacity. Best for modern aesthetic.

üßä Ice Maker Features

  • Craft Ice (LG LHFS28XBS): Unique round ice spheres that melt slowly - great for cocktails
  • Nugget Ice: Soft, chewable ice (Samsung models at Lowe's)
  • Dual/Triple: More ice production capacity, less running out
