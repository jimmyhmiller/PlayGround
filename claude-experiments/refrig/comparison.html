Comparison Notes

  • Counter Depth vs Standard Depth: Counter depth models (like Samsung) fit flush with cabinets but have less capacity. Standard depth models (like LG) extend beyond counters but offer more storage.
  • Dimensions: All three are approximately 35.75" wide, fitting standard 36" cabinet openings.
  • Height: All around 70" tall - make sure you have clearance.
  • Energy: All are Energy Star certified with similar consumption (726-731 kWh/year).
