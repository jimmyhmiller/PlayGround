Common questions

The icon isn't showing up in my menu bar.

Ease is a menu bar app — there is no Dock icon by default. After launching, look at the top-right of your screen. If the menu bar is full, macOS may have hidden Ease behind the overflow chevron.

How do I sync between Macs?

Sign in to iCloud on both Macs and make sure iCloud Drive is enabled. Ease uses your private CloudKit database — there's nothing to configure inside the app.

How do I clear my data?

Right-click the menu bar icon, hold ⌘⌥ (Command + Option) and choose Clear Data. This deletes your local data and the matching records in iCloud.

How do I export?

Right-click the menu bar icon and choose Export Heatmap…. A PNG of your activity is saved to the location you pick.
